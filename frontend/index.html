Built for creators, loved by communities

Every feature designed to help you share your best work and connect with the people who love it.

📸
Smart Upload
Drag-and-drop photo upload directly to Azure Blob Storage with 8 built-in CSS filters and rich metadata tagging.
👥
People Tagging
Tag people who appear in your photos with interactive pin labels. Azure Computer Vision assists with auto-detection.
❤️
Rich Reactions
Double-tap to like with a burst animation, star ratings, comments and bookmark to save photos for later.
🔍
Smart Discovery
Explore by hashtag, location, creator or keyword. Trending content curated by engagement metrics.
🔒
Role-Based Access
Separate creator and consumer roles via Azure AD B2C. Creators upload, consumers discover and engage.
⚡
Edge Performance
Azure Front Door CDN delivers photos from the nearest edge node globally. Sub-100ms load times.
📊
Creator Analytics
Track total likes, comments, average ratings and your top-performing photos from your Creator Studio.
🌙
Dark Mode
Full dark and light theme support across every page, persisted to your browser preference automatically.

How Lumora works

Get started in minutes. No credit card required.

1
Create your account
Sign up as a consumer to browse and engage, or ask an admin to grant you creator access.
2
Upload or Discover
Creators upload photos with metadata, filters and person tags. Consumers browse the feed and explore.
3
Like, Save & Comment
Engage with photos you love — rate them, leave comments, save to your collection and share links.
